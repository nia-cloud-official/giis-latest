About

ICOFF, headquartered in Zimbabwe, is a global leader in firefighting education, certification, and advocacy. For over two decades, we’ve empowered firefighters and emergency responders with world-class training, internationally recognized certifications, and a platform for collaboration.

Who are we?

Welcome to the Council of Fire Fighters (ICOFF)

The International Council of Fire Fighters (ICOFF), headquartered in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe, is a global leader dedicated to advancing firefighting as a profession through education, collaboration, and innovation. For over two decades, we’ve championed the interests of firefighters worldwide, setting rigorous industry standards and delivering cutting-edge certifications and training programs.

Our Mission

Advance the profession of fire fighting: Promote the interests of fire fighters worldwide and support their professional development.

Our Vision

Provide training and certification: Offer certifications, training programs, and resources for fire fighters to enhance their skills and knowledge.

Core Values

Academic Excellence

We strive for excellence in education and training, ensuring that our programs meet the highest standards and prepare firefighters for real-world challenges.

Community Engagement

We actively engage with communities to promote safety, awareness, and collaboration in firefighting and emergency response efforts.

Innovation

We embrace creativity and forward-thinking approaches to tackle challenges and improve firefighting techniques and education.

Global Perspective

We foster a global outlook, encouraging collaboration and knowledge-sharing among firefighting professionals worldwide to address challenges and improve safety standards.

Connecting people

Empowering Future Leaders in Firefighting

At ICOFF, we believe in nurturing leadership qualities and empowering individuals to make a difference in the firefighting community. Our programs are designed to inspire, educate, and prepare future leaders to tackle challenges with confidence and innovation.

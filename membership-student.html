Student Enrolment

Begin your journey as a professional firefighter or emergency responder. Enroll at ICOFF and access world-class training, mentorship, and career opportunities.

Enrolment Open: 2025

ICOFF Student Enrolment Form

The International Council of Fire Fighters (ICOFF) is a leading fire and emergency services school. Our student enrolment is open to anyone passionate about fire safety, rescue, and emergency response. Whether you are starting your career or seeking to advance your skills, ICOFF provides internationally recognized training and support.

Why Enroll at ICOFF?

  • World-class firefighting and emergency response training
  • Internationally recognized certifications and credentials
  • Hands-on practical experience and physical training
  • Mentorship from experienced instructors
  • Career support and job placement assistance
  • Access to a global network of fire professionals

ICOFF welcomes students from all backgrounds. Our programs are designed for both beginners and those with experience in fire and emergency services.

To enroll, please complete the application form below. Our admissions team will contact you with next steps and further requirements.

Have questions? Contact us for more information about enrolment and programs.

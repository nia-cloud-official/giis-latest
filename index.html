Welcome to the, Global Institute of Intergrates Studies

At GIIS, we believe education should be transformative, not transactional. Nestled in the vibrant city of Bulawayo, Zimbabwe, our institute is a launchpad for passionate individuals ready to shape the future of wellness, beauty, tourism, hospitality, business and many more.

Whether you're dreaming of curating unforgettable travel experiences, mastering the art of therapeutic care, or leading in luxury guest services, GIIS is where your journey begins.

Our Mission

We aim to equip our students with the theoretical knowledge and practical experience necessary to meet the evolving challenges of multiple industries.

Our Vision

GIIS fosters a global network of professionals committed to excellence in several fields.

Our Values

- Respect for self, others, and the environment.
- Integrity in actions and academics.
- Collaboration across differences.

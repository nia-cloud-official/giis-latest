ICOFF Certifications

At the International Council of Fire Fighters (ICOFF), we are committed to delivering globally aligned, industry-relevant certifications that empower individuals with the skills, confidence, and credibility to operate effectively in high-risk fire and emergency environments. Each certification is structured to meet international standards and tailored to address the unique challenges faced by fire services in various regions.

All our training programs are delivered by qualified professionals, grounded in real-world practices, and aligned with internationally recognized firefighting and emergency response frameworks.

Certificate in Basic Firefighting and Rescue

  • Duration: 4 – 6 Weeks
  • Mode: On-site (Theory & Practical)
  • Credential Awarded: ICOFF Certificate of Completion
  • Ideal For: Entry-level firefighters, emergency responders, security personnel, municipal recruits, airport fire staff

Course Overview: This foundational course prepares students to handle essential firefighting operations with competence and discipline. It covers fire science, equipment usage, fireground safety, rescue techniques, and basic hazardous materials awareness. Students undergo physical training and practical simulations to develop readiness for real-world incidents.

  • Fire Behavior & Fire Science
  • Use of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
  • Hose Handling & Fire Suppression
  • Basic Rescue & Search Techniques
  • Fire Extinguishers & Scene Control
  • Firefighter Safety & Risk Awareness

Upon completion, students receive a Certificate in Basic Firefighting and Rescue and may proceed to advanced training or apply for public or private sector emergency service roles.

Certificate in Fire Safety and Prevention (1 Week Intensive)

  • Duration: 5 Days (Full-time)
  • Credential Awarded: ICOFF Certificate of Competence
  • Ideal For: Fire wardens, safety officers, building managers, NGO workers, school staff

Course Overview: This one-week program focuses on proactive fire safety measures and prevention strategies in homes, workplaces, and public institutions. It is ideal for professionals responsible for ensuring fire safety compliance and conducting fire risk assessments.

  • Principles of Fire Safety
  • Fire Risk Assessment and Mitigation
  • Evacuation Planning and Emergency Procedures
  • Safe Use of Fire Extinguishers
  • Electrical, Kitchen, and Workplace Hazards
  • Conducting Fire Drills

Graduates of this course are equipped to serve as fire safety officers or fire wardens within their organizations.

Certificate in Emergency Scene Management (1 Week Intensive)

  • Duration: 1 Week
  • Credential Awarded: ICOFF Certificate of Completion
  • Ideal For: Team leaders, supervisors, first responders, incident managers

Course Overview: This short course is designed to strengthen leadership and coordination during emergency incidents. It introduces students to incident command systems, tactical decision-making, communication in high-pressure environments, and coordinated response operations.

  • Incident Command System (ICS)
  • Team-Based Fireground Operations
  • Communication & Leadership under Stress
  • Command and Control Protocols
  • Post-Incident Reporting and Debriefing

Certificate in First Aid and Trauma Support for Firefighters

  • Duration: 3 – 5 Days
  • Credential Awarded: ICOFF First Aid & Trauma Certificate
  • Ideal For: Firefighters, responders, community volunteers, NGO field workers

Overview: This course provides essential life-saving knowledge on managing casualties before paramedics arrive. It is focused on fire-related injuries, smoke inhalation, trauma, bleeding, and CPR.

Why Choose ICOFF Certification?

  • International Credibility: All courses are designed to align with NFPA, IFSAC, and globally recognized standards.
  • Career-Ready Skills: Our graduates are prepared for real-world emergency work.
  • Professional Recognition: Certificates issued by ICOFF are respected across public and private fire service networks.
  • Accessible Entry Points: From beginners to professionals, our training is built for progression.

Certification Requirements

  • Attend a minimum of 90% of training sessions
  • Successfully pass theory and practical evaluations
  • Demonstrate physical readiness (where applicable)
  • Abide by ICOFF’s code of discipline and conduct

Certificates are issued digitally and in printed format, complete with serial numbers and verification codes.

Verifying an ICOFF Certificate

Employers and institutions may verify ICOFF credentials by contacting our verification office directly or using our secure verification portal (coming soon). For certificate validation, please email: certificates@icoff.org
